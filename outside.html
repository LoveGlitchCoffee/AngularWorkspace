Outside

Outside

A slightly unconventional zombie shooter in a tower-defense esque game. You assume the role of a grandpa protecting his grandchild with 'special' walking sticks. Shoot the zombies before they destroy your barriers and get to you and your family. Featuring a short storyline, 5 progressive levels, 3 different weapons and some explosion + screenshake, Outside presents a concise and delightful experience.


My Story

This was not a game where I wanted to test out some new mechanic or learn some new skills. This game was my test. I wanted to see if I could really make a game that is sufficiently polished, detailed and finished. Its not at professional level of course and I did not make all the assets, but as a game I think this is something that people would play and hopefully enjoy. It is one of the few games I can call finish so I am quite proud of this project.


My role and contribution

I did all the programming. I did use a messeging system that was being experimented by a friend, but they did not like it so I took over.

Most of the model are from the Unity asset store and online. There are so much thanks to go around of course (Thank you M.eye in particular for the zombie model). Only the drawings of the walking sticks in the weapon selection menu was my work, along with several other textures like health bar/special bar etc.
