Unnamed Stealth Game

Unnamed Stealth Game

A stealth-platformer, where the player’s goal is to get to the next room. The player must find a way to get past the guards and find the key. There are multiple ways to get past the guards, either through sheer skill or utilising guard’s ‘weaknesses’.

This was my first attempt at making a proper game, having attained appropriate skill level and understanding of scope. The game is has only 2 levels and despite there being multiple ways to pass a level, it is relatively linear.

My Story

I programmed all parts of the game in Unity3D as this was an individual project. I am really fascinated with game AI, hence this was my 'golden child'. The guards can perform Breadth First Search as seen in video 2.
