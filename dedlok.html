Ded-Lok

Ded-Lok

A simple shooter. You aim is to shoot the enemy whilst avoiding their ‘arrows’. The game was written in Chip8, an assembly language that was used for the old COSMAC VIP. It is an entry to Octojam and can be seen as an entry here octo-jam. The assembler, Octo, can be used to play the game from your browser, click Ded-Lok


My Story

I was introduced to octojam by a friend. When I read about Chip8, I felt challenged and excited. Chip8 only has 16 registers to work with, only 35 instructions to use and a limit of 3.5kb, as the COSMAC VIP was so old. I was able to pull it off in 2 days, resulting in Ded-Lok, a 468 byte game.
