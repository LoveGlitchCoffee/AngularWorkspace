Ghostsweeper

Ghostsweeper, the Horror-Puzzle Mobile Game

A puzzle mobile game inspired by Minesweeper. The player’s goal is to find the holy cross (last-image). Players can tap on each tile to reveal what’s under it. This could be an arrow that points to the direction of the holy cross or a number, indicating how many 'mines' are around the tile (identical to minesweeper).


My Story

I was the sole programmer in a team of 3 whilst doing an internship at Guava7 (defunct), therefore responsible for all programming implementation of the game. The game was completed in 5 weeks for both the App Store and the Play Store. As Guava7 has become defunct however, Ghostsweeper was taken off the App store and Play Store.
